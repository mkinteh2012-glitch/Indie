// The Brief

Indie is a challenge for high schoolers who want to see their work on a global stage. Getting a game on Steam costs $100 USD—we're here to cover that for you.

The mission: Start a project, track your coding via Hackatime, and ship a playable build. Creating art or music? You can use Lapse to track your creative time too. Once your team hits the hour goal, we fund your Steam Developer License.

// The Math

You can ship solo or in teams of up to 4. Each extra person adds +5 hours to the base 30h goal:
